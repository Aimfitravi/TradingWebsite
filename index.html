Why Trading

Trading involves buying and selling financial instruments, such as stocks, currencies, commodities, or cryptocurrencies, with the aim of profiting from short-term price fluctuations. Traders use various strategies, including technical and fundamental analysis, to predict price movements and make rapid buying or selling decisions. Trading can be highly profitable, but it also carries significant risk due to market volatility, and success often depends on an individual's knowledge, skills, and ability to manage risk effectively.
